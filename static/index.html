📊 About Swapify Health Score

Every product starts neutral and earns its score — nothing is assumed. Here's exactly how each part of the label adds up:

ComponentWhat it meansEffect on score
⚖️Base Score Neutral starting point for every product Starts at 5.0
🍬Sugar Penalty Docked when sugar per serving is high −1 to −2 pts
🧂Sodium Penalty Docked when sodium per serving is high −1 to −2 pts
🥓Saturated Fat Penalty Docked when saturated fat per serving is high −1 to −2 pts
💪Protein Bonus Rewarded when protein per serving is high +1 to +2 pts
🌾Fiber Bonus Rewarded when fiber per serving is high +1 to +2 pts
⚠️Ingredient Penalties Harmful ingredients — palm oil, MSG, artificial dyes, and more −0.5 to −0.8 each
🔍Transparency Multiplier Vague labelling (e.g. just "flavours") nudges the final score down ×0.95–×1.05
🎯Final Score Everything above, combined Your health score
